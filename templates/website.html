Visualizations

The calendar and the map

Calendar

There is a color spectrum based on the number of accidents that occur on a specific day. As you hover over each calendar sqaure you will see details of the accidents from that day, and if you select a specific day of the calendar by clicking on it, a world tour will appear and show you the country of origin.

Map

The map shows a cluster of points based on accidents that have occured around the world
